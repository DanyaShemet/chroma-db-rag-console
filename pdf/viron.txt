=Viron is a fictional exoplanet located in the Epsilon Eridani system.

It has a dense purple atmosphere composed mainly of argon and unknown organic particles.

The surface temperature averages -20 degrees Celsius, but geothermal activity creates warm zones.

Viron has three moons: Tala, Veris, and Omra.

Exploration missions have reported strange electromagnetic signals believed to originate from underground structures.
