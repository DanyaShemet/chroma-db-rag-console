Aurelion is a fictional floating city located above the Pacific Ocean.

The city was founded in 2145 by engineer Loran Veyr. It floats using massive anti-gravity crystals called "Zephyrites".

Aurelion has a population of approximately 120,000 people. The main source of energy is solar wind conversion.

The city is governed by a council of seven engineers known as the "Core Minds".

Every year, Aurelion celebrates "Skyfall Day" on March 18, marking the anniversary of its first successful lift-off.
